PlaceBeaver

A quick and simple service for getting pictures of beaver for use as placeholders in your designs or code.
Just put your image size (width & height) after our URL and you'll get a placeholder.
Optionally pass a 3rd param to set the compression, default is 75.

Like this: http://placebeaver.com/200/300

Inspired by placekitten.com - Powered by just 37 lines of PHP, check out the GitHub repo.
